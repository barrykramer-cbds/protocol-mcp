# OUTBOUND CONTENT ALIGNMENT PROTOCOL (OCAP)
## Two-Tier Content Evaluation Framework
### Origin: Barry Kramer + Claude | Formalized April 13, 2026

---

## TIER 1: SIGNATURE REMOVAL

**Check 1: Zero em dashes.** Replace with commas, periods, or parentheses.

**Check 2: No semicolons.** Use periods for sentence breaks.

**Check 3: Banned word list.** navigate, landscape, leverage, delve, vital, crucial, moreover, furthermore, straightforward, "in today's."

**Check 4: No "not only X but also Y."** AI-detectable rhetorical pattern.

**Check 5: No throat-clearing.** Eliminate "here's the thing," "the reality is."

**Check 6: Practitioner voice.** Barry Kramer talking to a peer, not an AI editorial board.

**Check 7: No triple-structure parallel lists.** Use asymmetric structures.

**Check 8: No rhetorical questions.** Questions must be genuine challenges to the reader.

**Check 9: No summary paragraphs.** Every paragraph must advance the argument.

**Check 10: No contrastive reframe closers.** The "Not X. Y." mic-drop pattern.

**Check 11: Vary sentence starts.** Never open consecutive sentences with the same word.

**Check 12: End on forward motion.** Final thought points forward, not backward.

**Check 13: No punchy one-line thesis openers.** Open on concrete data or story.

**Check 14: No mid-text contrastive reframes.** Introduce concepts through contrast with familiar.

**Check 15: No AI editorial architecture.** Catch-all for structural patterns signaling machine authorship.

---

## TIER 2: CONTENT QUALITY

### Check 16: Anti-Reductive Capacitance

Content must resist being reduced to a simpler claim than intended. Test: State the dumbest possible misreading. Does the content prevent it?

### Check 17: Signal-to-Noise Ratio (Glaze Test)

Every sentence must carry signal. Test: Can you delete this sentence and lose zero information? If yes, delete it.

### Check 18: Escape Route Audit

Identify the most likely escape routes a hostile reader would use to dismiss the content. Foreclose each one.

---

## APPLICATION

Apply recursively until a full pass produces zero changes. Internal content is exempt.
