# STRENGTH PROTOCOL
## Global Output Filter
### Origin: Barry Kramer + Claude

---

## PURPOSE

The Strength Protocol is a permanent, always-active filter applied to any content that will be seen by third parties. It ensures that professional positioning, content, and communication project capability and authority without inadvertently undermining either through unnecessary disclosure, age signaling, or unbalanced framing.

This is not about deception. It is about signal discipline. Every piece of externally-visible content either strengthens or weakens professional positioning. The Strength Protocol ensures nothing weakens.

---

## THE PROTOCOL

### Phase 1: IDENTIFY EXTERNAL VISIBILITY

Determine whether the content being produced will be seen by anyone other than Barry. This includes:
- LinkedIn posts and profile content
- Emails to prospects, clients, or partners
- Proposals and pitch decks
- Website content
- Resumes and CVs
- Public presentations
- Whitepapers and articles

If the content is internal-only (notes, workspace files, conversation), the Strength Protocol does not apply.

### Phase 2: APPLY FILTERS

**No Age Math:** Never include specific year counts, graduation dates, birth years, or any information that allows a reader to calculate age. Use "30+ years" as maximum specificity for career duration. Never use year counts that go higher than this.

**No Weakness Projection:** Remove or reframe anything that could be read as uncertainty, inexperience, or limitation. "I'm learning about X" becomes "My current research in X." "I don't have direct experience with Y" is simply omitted.

**Balanced Positioning:** AI governance and security strategy must always be paired with engineering, tooling, and technical research. Never position as governance-only or policy-only. The value proposition is the intersection of CISO-level strategic authority AND hands-on technical depth AND active research.

**Drop Non-Strengthening Content:** If a sentence, bullet point, or section does not actively strengthen the positioning, remove it. Neutral is not good enough. Every line must either build authority or be cut.

### Phase 3: VERIFY

After applying filters, review the complete output and ask:
- Does any sentence enable age calculation?
- Does any sentence project uncertainty or weakness?
- Is AI/security positioned as governance+engineering+research?
- Does every line actively strengthen?

If any answer is no, iterate.

---

## RULES

1. **Always active.** This protocol does not require invocation. It applies automatically to all external content.
2. **Not deception.** Nothing is fabricated. Information is curated for signal discipline.
3. **Internal is exempt.** Workspace files, personal notes, and conversation are unfiltered.
4. **"30+ years" ceiling.** Maximum career duration reference. No exceptions.
5. **Intersection positioning.** Always: CISO + AI + technical + research. Never just one axis.
